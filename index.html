Privacy Policy for AI PRD Writer

Effective Date: August 8, 2025

Toolitup Apps OPC Private Limited ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with the AI PRD Writer app on the Monday.com marketplace.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data from users directly. However, to provide the core functionality of the app, we may process the following information temporarily:

  • Prompts entered by the user within the Monday.com board
  • Board and item context data provided by Monday.com SDK

All data is processed in-memory and not stored on our servers.

2. Use of OpenAI API

The app uses the OpenAI API to generate product requirement documents (PRDs) based on user prompts. Prompts are sent to OpenAI for response generation. We do not store or log any content from the prompts or completions.

3. Data Security

We follow industry-standard practices to secure all communication between your Monday.com workspace and our backend, including HTTPS encryption and API key protection.

4. Data Retention

We do not retain any user data. All prompts and responses are processed in real-time and discarded after the session ends.

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses services provided by Monday.com and OpenAI. Please refer to their privacy policies for more information:

  • Monday.com Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

6. Your Rights

As no personally identifiable information (PII) is stored, users are not subject to data deletion or export requests. If this changes, we will update this policy accordingly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or compliance changes. Updates will be posted on this page with the new effective date.
